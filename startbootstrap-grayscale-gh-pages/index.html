Our Work

There is a thread of life existing through all things, it is a sacred thread that creates all of life. It holds its own desires, intentions, designs, and is the language of life; its conversation, if you will, offered to us as the Living System. This vital construct of life mostly goes unnoticed and without voice, yet it is a space we must all return to in order to truly ‘unravel and return’, a process that asks each of us to recollect our truth, and honour all that we are as custodians on this great planet.

We will never take up the call to the challenges we are facing, without truly recognising our place and purpose amongst it.

This is the work as a book, a conversation and as a meandering course, to once more remind each of us of the Living System, of our sacredness, of our part and purpose in the reweaving of all things to wholeness and to reveal the loose ties we can knit, through which we will ‘make way’ together, as One Living System.
